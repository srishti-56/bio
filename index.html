Longer explanation:
E.C.S.T.A.T.I.C.S (Enabling Civilian Security through Adaptive Tracking and Intelligent Computer Systems) was a computer vision startup I co-founded. We developed a real-time 'abandoned luggage' tracking solution for an autonomous network of surveillance camera systems at airports and stations, leveraging OpenCV and TinyYOLO models. The system could dynamically mesh network the cameras and alert security personnel through the closest node, enabling rapid response to potential threats and improving civilian safety in public spaces.
